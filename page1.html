Waste Collection

This Service provides you doorstep facility of Waste Collection from anywhere keeping in mind the individual's Convenience . Waste disposal includes the processes and actions required to manage waste from its inception to its final disposal.The individual provide waste to the collector which they want to be get recycled .The Wastes are then segregated into two types : Bio-degradable and Non-Biodegradable waste .Online recycling System includes the Hand-On waste collection with the ERP Services ,the waste gets collected by the Municipal Corporation and it gets recycled with useful products .Various Products made from recycled waste are Bike tyres , tough fibres , Mats , Decorative wares , etc .
